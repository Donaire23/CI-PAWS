Animals Need Your Help

We need your financial support in order to put a stop to cruelty to animals. Together, we can make the world a better place for all beings. Your donation will go to work instantly to help animals suffering and dying on factory farms and in laboratories, the fur industry, and circuses as well as other forms of "entertainment." Whether you are a long-time CI-PAWS supporter or you are joining for the first time, thank you for everything you do to help animals.

CI-PAWS is a non-profit organisation that does not receive any kind of help or subsidy for our work. That is why, to be able to continue giving second chances to stray animals, we need your donation today.

The Philippines is a country full of natural beauty and scenery, but hidden behind the idyllic beauty is an entirely different reality for animals. Abandonment, abuse, cruelty and the situation worsens with the natural misfortunes that plague this country year after year. Every year the number of animals grows and our resources cannot keep up, that is why we ask you to make a donation that allows us to continue making this project a reality.

How do we use your donations?

Cover expenses for veterinary medical care so that the animals can recover and stay healthy.

Buy food and supplements that are required to feed the 175 rescued animals on a daily basis.

Salaries for local employees that are attending to the animals' needs and the facilities, with which they can support their families.
